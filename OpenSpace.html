How does the interface work?

Build your own rocket or select a pre existing one
Change settings like time factor and/or change your point of view
Rocket Dashboard: Control your Rocket and keep track of fuel and throttle
Turn sound on or off

Key Assignment:

space = throttle
w = x rotation
s = x rotation
a = z rotation
d = z rotation
q = y rotation
e = y rotation
